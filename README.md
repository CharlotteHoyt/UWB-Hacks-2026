# I'm a Human Too
This is our project for UWB Hacks: The Future. We submitted to the Human Experience track. 

When brainstorming ideas for a project related to "the future," themes that frequently came up included technology/AI, space, and climate change. Our submission, a game, combines these ideas.

Facing the collapse of Earth, citizens line up for interrogation to demonstrate their humanness and earn their spot on a spaceship to a nearby habitable planet. The player is in charge of administering questions about the human experience but must be careful who is admitted onto the spaceship since a spot for a bot means one spot less for a human.
